Our Services

Electrical Consultancy

Expert advice and guidance for electrical installations

Ramesh Electricals provides expert consultancy services, ensuring electrical systems are efficient, safe, and compliant with regulations. They assess existing infrastructures, offer load analysis, and recommend energy-saving improvements. Their tailored consultancy serves industries, IT parks, hospitals, hotels, residential complexes, and commercial buildings, ensuring optimal performance and adherence to industry standards.

Electrical Panel Fabrication

Designing and manufacturing custom electrical panels

Ramesh Electricals fabricates custom electrical panels designed to meet specific load demands and ensure safe power distribution. Their panels are built using high-quality components, rigorously tested for durability and efficiency. These solutions cater to various sectors, including industries, commercial buildings, and residential complexes, providing reliable, long-lasting, and easy-to-maintain control panels.

Fire Fighting System

Installation and maintenance of fire safety systems

Ramesh Electricals offers comprehensive fire fighting systems that include fire detection, alarms, and suppression solutions. These systems are customized for industries, IT parks, hospitals, hotels, and residential complexes. Designed to comply with fire safety regulations, they provide early hazard detection and effective suppression, ensuring protection of lives and property from fire risks.

Service, Design, and Detailed Drawings

Comprehensive electrical designs and schematics for all building types

They provide detailed designs and drawings for electrical systems, tailored to project specifications and safety standards. The comprehensive schematics ensure seamless installation and operation. Ramesh Electricals also offers post-installation services, including maintenance and updates, ensuring long-term system reliability and performance across various industries and building types.
